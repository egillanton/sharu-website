About Us

Student Hackathon Association at Reykjavik University (NHVHR) organizes hackathons for any university/college student. At these events, these students gather together to collaborate, innovate, learn, and experiment while creating a project from scratch within 24 hours.

SHARU will provide students all the resources they need, from hardware and APIs to workshops, sponsors, and volunteers who can help you with whatever questions the students might have. In a few words, you come here to these events to have fun, learn about things you didn’t even know existed, and see your ideas come to life. We encourage all college or university students from any faculty to participate in our events.

If you are are interesting organizing or becoming a volunteer, please dont hesitate to contact us.
